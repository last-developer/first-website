Our Methods

Positive reinforcement training

This method of training involves rewarding your dog for good behavior with treats, praise, or playtime. It focuses on reinforcing desirable behaviors rather than punishing unwanted behavior.

Relationship-based training

This method of training focuses on building a strong bond between the owner and the dog. It involves establishing trust, communication, and mutual respect with the dog.
